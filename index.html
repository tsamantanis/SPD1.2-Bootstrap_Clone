Reimagining the page

Designed for better writing and thinking.

Word processors have always worked in straight lines – top to bottom, left to right. But that’s not how our minds work. What if we had a tool that could work in straight lines or… well… not straight lines? We think it makes for a pretty powerful thinking tool.

We built a new style of text editor from the ground up to better support creative thinking. Clover lets you explode traditional documents and work in a more free-form manner for better brainstorming, mind-mapping, and exploration.

From idea to reality

Manage your tasks.
Plan your days.

Ideas are nothing without execution. Clover integrates tasks directly within your documents to make project management as simple as writing. The Agenda view lets you track notes and tasks per day, so you never forget anything.

A command line for creativity

Quickly capture ideas, notes, and tasks.

We know ideas can strike at any moment, so we made it as frictionless as possible to capture things as they pop up. Open Clover's command prompt with a simple keystroke, and it will interpret what you write to automatically log notes and schedule tasks for you so you can get back to work.

Customizable

Make it yours.

Notebooks are personal. You should be able to tailor them to fit your tastes. We built Clover on a flexible style engine so you can customize the color scheme, typography, and icons to create a workspace as unique as you.
